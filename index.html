About Out AND About Marin

Out AND About Marin was built by a Marin County mom who was tired of spending more time searching for things to do than actually doing them. Every week I found myself bouncing between library websites, rec center calendars, neighborhood Facebook groups, and community newsletters — just trying to figure out what was happening for families in our own backyard. There had to be a better way. So I built one.

This app is new, and growing and improving every week. If something is missing, wrong, or could be better — I genuinely want to know. So use the Feedback button below or join the WhatsApp group and tell me.
